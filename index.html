My Learning Journey

As I previously said, I am currently attending UVA with a potential major in Computer Science. I graduated from Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology with an Advanced Studies Diploma. In high school, I enrolled in AP CS, then Mobile & Web Development Courses, and then Artificial Intelligence.
